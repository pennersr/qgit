Extra Info

After submitting your details via the form below, you will be subscribed to the Ambassador newsletter, and you will automatically be sent the latest edition. Via this newsletter we communicate all the important Ambassador news, including the dates and locations for the Ambassador meetings. At the Ambassador meeting you will be provided with the Ambassador manual.

The manual tells you how many tickets you need to sell in order to qualify as an official Ambassador, how we support you promote and sell tickets, what rewards you earn for selling tickets, and all other important info you need to know.

If you still have questions after reading the info section and receiving the Ambassador newsletter, you can contact us via email here.

If you have previously subscribed as an Ambassador, you can update your (contact) details here .
