Important Information

Roadworks
Because of roadworks, the A12 to Utrecht will be closed between Veenendaal and Maarsbergen, after Qlimax. Best is to take a detour via the A325 – A15 and A2, for drivers to Utrecht and further.

Sold Out
Qlimax is sold out. The advice is not to buy any tickets via the illegal circuit, as there is a big chance they are fake.
Attention! Your ticket will indicate an entrance (E, Q or VIP). You have to enter via the entrance as stated on your ticket. You cannot enter the event via any other entrance.

Times
The doors of the GelreDome open at 9PM and Qlimax will be finished at 7AM Sunday morning. The first DJ set starts at 10PM, so make sure you're on time to not miss anything of the show!

Timetable
The timetable for Qlimax will not be released. But you can check the order of the acts performing in the line-up presentation at the Qlimax website .

The weather - cold
The weather forecast for the weekend: it's going to be quite cold. So be prepared and take warm clothing with you!

Anthem
This year’s Qlimax anthem is produced by Zatox – No Way Back and is available for download at Soundcloud.com/Qdance .

Blu-Ray - DVD - CD
Also this year, a digipack including a Blu-Ray, DVD and CD will be released of the event. The bonus-CD will contain the liveset from Headhunterz.

Qlimax will be the first event in The Netherlands which will only be filmed by RED filmcameras in a multicam set-up.

Register here to secure yourself a copy, and be the first one to be informed about any news about this year’s Qlimax digipack.

Only via the Q-dance Merchandise Store , you will be given an exclusive Q-dance gadget when ordering.

Buses
Just for Qlimax, there are 152 locations where you can hop on a bus to go to Qlimax. Check here at what times the buses depart from the different locations in The Netherlands. You can order your busticket at eventtravel.nl .

If you are travelling with 15 or more persons, you can also book a private bus. You can then also pick your own pick-up location.

Advantages of travelling by bus are:
- you're on time at the party!
- no need to stay sober as you're not driving back yourself
- you can start the party already in the bus!
- parking is close to the entrance.

Arranged your own bus?
With events at the GelreDome, there is no need to register your bus before the event. If your bus is just dropping off, you do not need to pay any parking fees. A parking fee of EUR 40,- applies if the bus will be parked. Read more info about buses and parking here.

Cameras & Registration
Like any other Q-dance event, at Qlimax all types of camera's will be allowed into the event. So also digital SLR equipment.
Use hashtag #QL11 when sharing your photos and videos.

Of course you can film every show moment during Qlimax, it’s your party.
But we’ld like to advice to enjoy all the shows to the max! We’ll upload movies of the showmoments to Youtube.com/Qdancebackstage , shortly after Qlimax.

Shortly after the event, you can find a selection of the best photos and the film at the Q-dance website.

CD
The Qlimax CD, mixed by Zatox, is now available in the Q-dance Merchandise Store .
During Qlimax the CD will also be available at the Free Record Shop stand.

Drugs
We must comply with the local drug policy in Arnhem, which is zero-tolerance. A lot of people do not know what a zero-tolerance policy means; it means that the Arnhem council is not allowing any forms of soft and/or harddrugs. So officialy you are not even allowed to take any weed with you inside the event.

Hashtag
For everyone using Twitter: use the keyword #QL11 when writing about Qlimax.
Let’s make #QL11 a trending topic!

ID and age
As usual there will be strict age and identity checks at the entrance. So make sure you bring your ID (drivers license, ID-only, passport) so your entrance won't be refused. A copy of your ID is NOT valid. The minimum age for visiting Qlimax is 18 years.

Lockers
There is no cloakroom at Qlimax. Instead you can use the available lockers. Hiring a locker for the night is EUR 6,- and EUR 5,- deposit.
You can easily fit 3 coats in a locker, so please share with your friends.

If you have a regular ticket, access to your locker during the event won't be allowed. VIP ticket holders will have their lockers in the VIP area, and these are accessible during the event.

Merchandise
Also this year a new collection Qlimax merchandise has been made. Prevent dissapointments and make sure you find your way to the merchandise stand early on the night. There will be an EFTPOS machine at the stand, but of course you can also pay with CASH.

Public transport
You can easily travel to Qlimax by train. There will be a shuttle bus service from station Arnhem to GelreDome. Check www.ns.nl for a trip planner from door-to-door. Be smart, and buy a weekend-return at the ticket machine. For more info about all public transport visit the website of the GelreDome (click on route).

Radio Live-broadcast
Qlimax will be broadcast LIVE at Q-dance Radio . All artist sets will be broadcast and Fausto will be hosting the radio show, together with André.
The LIVE-broadcast starts at 9PM till 7AM Sundaymorning (GMT+1).

Take care of yourself ... and each other
A whole night of partying requires more energy than you might be aware of. Make sure you come to the event well prepared. Drink and sleep enough and keep in mind that it will be cold outside. Finally, our motto is "Come together, stay together ", so take care of your friends and yourself.

VIP
Q-dancers who bought a VIP ticket, will receive the following:
- Seperate entrance (follow signing VIP)
- VIP bracelet
- Free locker
- Entrance to the VIP tribune
- Entrance to the VIP Lounge
Check the timetable of the VIP Lounge here .
